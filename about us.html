nikhil coconut.com was established in the year 2021 as per the company act 2013 with an objective to provide the best benefits of coconuts to customer. The company aims for the integrated development of the farmers by maximizing their income through better agro management practices and promoting coconut cropping and farming ecosystems. The registered coconut farmer will get benefits in the form of finance, insurance, subsidies, farm training, crop saplings, etc. The company has 8 board of directors for 8 bhigwan and is now expanded to the neighboring districts pune and baramati. Now the head office is at takali and the branch office is at pune. The company has already acquired 8-acre lands for processing units at bhigwan and for the plant project which is already approached for Alfa Laval Company. Currently, more than 80 employees are working at all taluks of pune district as well as out of countries for marketing. By working together with farmers to support sustainable healthy farming and its products helps the company to make each coconut components reach the right destination.
